Biography

I received the M.S. degree in electrical and information engineering from Nanjing University of Science and Technology, Nanjing, China, in 2016, and Ph.D. degree in School of Information Science and Engineering, Southeast University, Nanjing, China, in 2021. I was the recipient of the Outstanding Ph.D. Thesis Award of Chinese Institute of Electronics in 2021.

I have a strong background and track record in physical neural network, wireless communication theory, and statistical signal processing estimation theory and have strong research skills including deep learning, convex optimization theory, robust optimization theory, discrete optimization theory, and fractional optimization theory.
